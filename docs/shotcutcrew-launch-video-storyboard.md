# ShotcutCrew Launch Video Storyboard

## Format

- **Video:** 45-second vertical reel
- **Canvas:** 1080 x 1920
- **Audience:** Clients booking shoots and production support
- **Language:** English
- **Style:** Cinematic, fast, premium marketplace feel
- **Use cases:** Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, WhatsApp launch share, website/social teaser
- **Palette:** ShotcutCrew orange, white, black, warm production lighting

## Core Message

ShotcutCrew helps clients plan, assemble, and execute shoots by connecting them with verified creative professionals, studios, equipment vendors, and production support.

Avoid:

- legal escrow language
- blockchain or web3 claims
- fixed-price guarantees
- automatic payout or settlement claims

## Editing Direction

- Use quick cuts every 1-2 seconds in the first half.
- Use smooth zoom-ins for website screen recordings.
- Keep captions large, high-contrast, and readable on mobile.
- Use orange highlight bars or glow for key words.
- Use transitions sparingly: light sweep, soft zoom, quick push, whip pan.
- Keep music cinematic and confident, not overly dramatic.
- Use a short sound hit when the ShotcutCrew logo appears.
- End on a clean brand frame for at least 2 seconds.

## Export Settings

- Format: MP4
- Codec: H.264
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Captions: burned in
- Audio: music plus voiceover
- Safe margins: keep text inside central 80 percent of frame

## Mobile QA Checklist

- Text readable on a 6-inch phone screen.
- Logo visible and not cropped.
- CTA visible for at least 2 seconds.
- No claims about escrow, blockchain, guaranteed pricing, or automatic payout.
- Website URL spelled exactly: `shotcutcrew.com`
- Video works without sound because captions explain the story.
